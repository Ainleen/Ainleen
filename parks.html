Welcome to parks in MANGALORE

Discover the best parks in mangalore to relax and enjoy the future

kadri park

Kadri Park is one of the oldest and most popular parks in Mangalore. It's a great place for a family outing with beautiful greenery and a peaceful environment.

Tagore Park

Located in the heart of the city, Tagore Park is a beautiful spot for walking, jogging, and enjoying the outdoors. The park is also known for its well-maintained surroundings.
