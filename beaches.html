Explore the serene and beautiful beaches that Mangalore has to offer!

Top Beaches in Mangalore

1. Panambur Beach

Panambur Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Mangalore. It is known for its cleanliness, beautiful sunsets, and various activities like beach sports and boat rides.

  • Location: Near Mangalore Port
  • Activities: Water sports, Dolphin watching, Beach games
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March

2. Surathkal Beach

Surathkal Beach is located near the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) and is known for its peaceful atmosphere and scenic beauty.

  • Location: Surathkal, 20 km from Mangalore city
  • Activities: Swimming, Sunset watching
  • Best Time to Visit: November to February

3. Tannirbhavi Beach

Tannirbhavi Beach is a quieter and less crowded beach, ideal for those who want to enjoy a peaceful time by the sea. It is perfect for picnics and long walks.

  • Location: Tannirbhavi, Mangalore
  • Activities: Walking, Relaxing, Photography
  • Best Time to Visit: November to February

4. Someshwar Beach

Someshwar Beach is a perfect blend of beauty and tranquility. Known for the famous Someshwar Temple, the beach is also a perfect spot for a sunset view.

  • Location: 13 km from Mangalore
  • Activities: Swimming, Temple visit, Sunset views
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March

5. Ullal Beach

Ullal Beach is located in a peaceful area near the old fishing town of Ullal. It is an excellent beach for relaxation, and the nearby lighthouse adds to its beauty.

  • Location: Ullal, 10 km south of Mangalore
  • Activities: Lighthouse visit, Photography, Relaxing
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
